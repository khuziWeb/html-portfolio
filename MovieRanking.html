The Best Movies Accourding to Me

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

The Godfather (1972)

Directed by Francis Ford Coppola, this epic crime film is widely regarded as one of the greatest movies ever made. It tells the story of the powerful Italian-American crime family of Don Vito Corleone (Marlon Brando) and his reluctant son Michael (Al Pacino).

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

Directed by Frank Darabont and based on Stephen King's novella, this film is a powerful drama about hope, friendship, and perseverance. It stars Tim Robbins as Andy Dufresne, a banker wrongfully convicted of murder, and Morgan Freeman as Red, his fellow inmate and friend.

Pulp Fiction (1994)

Directed by Quentin Tarantino, this neo-noir black comedy crime film is known for its eclectic dialogue, non-linear narrative, and memorable characters. It weaves multiple interconnected stories involving Los Angeles mobsters, a pair of philosophical hitmen (John Travolta and Samuel L. Jackson), a boxer, and a gangster's wife.
